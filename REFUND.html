Welcome

 

CS Global Telecomm. offers a full refund for any service that has not yet been activated. If your service has been activated, you may request a refund within 30 days of activation. Refunds will be processed within 10 business days of receipt of your request.


CS Global Telecomm. offers a 100% refund on all orders that are cancelled within 7 days of the order date. After 7 days, no refunds will be issued.


Cancellations

You may cancel your service at any time by calling our customer service department. There is no cancellation fee for service that has not yet been activated. If your service has been activated, you will be charged a cancellation fee of $50.


To cancel your order, please contact CS Global Telecomm. customer service at +1 (917) 694-4799. You can also cancel your order online by logging into your account and clicking on the "Cancel Order" button.


Exceptions

The following orders are not eligible for refunds or cancellations:


  • Orders that have already been shipped
  • Orders that have been activated
  • Orders that have been used in any way


Contact Information

If you have any questions about our refund and cancellation policy, please contact CS Global Telecomm. customer service at +1 (917) 694-4799
