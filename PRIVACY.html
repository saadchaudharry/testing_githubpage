Welcome

CS Global Telecommunication respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal information that you share with us. We will only collect and use your personal information in accordance with this privacy policy.

What information do we collect?

We collect the following information from you:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Billing address
  • Payment information
  • IP address
  • Device information
  • Usage data

How do we use your information?

  • We use your information to provide you with the cable TV service that you have requested.
  • We use your information to communicate with you about your account, your service, and other matters related to CS Global Telecommunication .
  • We use your information to improve our services by analyzing your usage data and feedback.
  • We use your information to protect our legal rights, such as to prevent fraud and to enforce our terms of service.

Personal Information

Personal information is information that can be used to identify you, such as your name, address, phone number, email address, and credit card information. We collect personal information from you when you:

  • Register for our services
  • Make a purchase
  • Contact us
  • Use our website

How We Use Your Personal Information

We use your personal information to provide you with the services that you have requested, to improve our services, and to communicate with you. We may also use your personal information to:

  • Send you marketing materials, such as email newsletters
  • Conduct research and analysis
  • Comply with applicable laws and regulations
  • Protect our rights and property

Sharing Your Personal Information

We may share your personal information with third parties who help us provide our services, such as:

  • Our payment processors
  • Our customer service providers
  • Our marketing partners

We may also share your personal information if we are required to do so by law or if we believe that sharing is necessary to protect our rights or property, to prevent fraud, or to protect the safety of others.

Your Choices

You have the following choices regarding your personal information:

  • You can opt-out of receiving marketing materials by clicking on the "unsubscribe" link in any marketing email that you receive from us.
  • You can access and correct your personal information by logging into your account or by contacting us.
  • You can request that we delete your personal information by contacting us.

Security

We take steps to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, and disclosure. These steps include:

  • Using secure servers
  • Encrypting sensitive information
  • Limiting access to your personal information to employees who need to access it to provide you with services

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make any material changes to this Privacy Policy, we will notify you by email or through a notice on our website.
